Tool Selection

The tools you select will heavily impact your ability as a barber. Although not necessary, finding the best tools for you will be quite important. Don't settle on the cheapest clipper you can find thinking they're all the same. They aren't. The tools you use may not have the biggest impact on the quality of your work, but it is the most important factor in the experience you have and give to your client will have. This page will walk you through all the tools that will give you the confidence to give your clients your best work.

Tool Meaning
Clipper Most commonly used tool for cutting hair. Used in association with guards and combs.
Guard Covering used on cilppers to leave certain lengths of hair on the head cuts. Usually numbered 1-8.
Detailer Smaller clipper used for trimming short hair or detaiing (designs, line-ups)
Buffer Shaver. This tool leaves no hair behind but is not as precise as a razor.
Shears Scissors
