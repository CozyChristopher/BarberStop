What is Barberstop?

Barberstop is the one stop shop for anyone interested in Men's grooming. This site includes descriptions of tools that barbers use, embedded tutorials for cutting hair, a gallery of hairstyles for people to look at and recreate, and a historical component for anyone who wants to know how their male ancestors styled their heads.

About the Creator

Hi my name is Christopher. I am a 4th year student at McMaster University studying Computer Science. I have been cutting hair 8 years and have learned a great deal during this time. I made this website for a university credit but I hope that one day barbers from around the world will be using this website to learn and browse.
